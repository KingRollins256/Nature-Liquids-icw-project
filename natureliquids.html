Nature Liquids

Juicing is a way to consume fruits or vegetables in liquid form. You need a juicer, an appliance, to separate the pulp (fiber) of the fruit or vegetable from the liquid. The liquid is thin and easy to drink, and it has the vitamins and minerals from the fruits and vegetables. Juicing often involves using a machine to take out the juice from fresh fruits or vegetables. The liquid contains most of the vitamins, minerals and plant chemicals found in the fruit. But whole fruits and vegetables also have healthy fiber, which is lost during most juicing.

Benefits of Juice

  1. It can help you meet your daily recommended intake of fruits and vegetables, which can lower your risk of many chronic diseases.
  2. It can provide a variety of antioxidants, phytochemicals and enzymes that may have anti-inflammatory, anti-cancer and detoxifying effects.
  3. It can boost your immune system, energy levels and hydration.

Risks of juices

  1. It can be high in calories and sugar, which can lead to weight gain and blood sugar spikes if you drink too much or add sweeteners.
  2. It can lack fiber, which can affect your digestion, blood sugar control and satiety.
  3. It can expose you to harmful bacteria or pesticides if you use unwashed or non-organic produce.
